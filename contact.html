Frequently Asked Questions

What's your planning timeline?
▼
We typically recommend 3-6 months for weddings and 1-3 months for corporate events. However, we can accommodate rush requests. Contact us to discuss timelines that work for you.
Do you handle events outside Coimbatore?
▼
Yes! While based in Coimbatore, we've executed events across Tamil Nadu, Bangalore, Chennai, and other locations. Destination events are our specialty.
What does your end-to-end service include?
▼
Planning, decor design, vendor coordination, logistics, hospitality, entertainment, production, on-ground execution, and real-time day-of coordination. One team, everything covered.
How do you finalize pricing?
▼
Every event is unique. After understanding your vision, guest count, and requirements, we provide a detailed proposal with transparent pricing and flexible packages.
